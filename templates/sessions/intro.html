BigQuery Technical Guide & Workshop

A comprehensive 2-day technical deep dive.

Welcome to the Masterclass. This workshop covers the entire BigQuery lifecycle, from internal architecture and smart ingestion to advanced security governance and AI integration.

Workshop Syllabus

  • Section 1: Architecture & Internals: Compute/Storage separation, Colossus, Capacitor, and Dremel.
  • Section 2: Ingestion & Optimization: Batch vs Streaming, Query lifecycle, and Slot management.
  • Section 3: Explore & Analyze: BI Tools, Data Profiling, Gemini, and Conversational Analytics.
  • Section 4: Governance & Security: IAM Hierarchy, Data Mesh, Column-level security, and DevOps.
  • Section 5: Cost Management: Pricing models (Editions), Slot autoscaling, and Monitoring.
  • Section 6: BigQuery AI: BigQuery ML, Gemini integration, and Data Canvas.
